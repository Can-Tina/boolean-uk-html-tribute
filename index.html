Ace Prosecutor Miles Edgeworth

Achievements

  • Joined the District Prosecutor's Office at the incredibly young age of 20
  • Maintained a perfect win record until he met Wright in court in 2016
  • Didn't murder anyone on a boat
  • Received the King of Prosecutors trophy
  • Became Chief Prosecutor

Personality

Cold, cool, and, collected, Miles Edgeworth once known as the Demon King of Prosecutors. However, there is more than meets the eye. He has also been shown to be caring and compassionate. In the trial of Will Powers, he is shown to realise the truth and help to get the real murderer behind bars

Iconic Outfits

Edgeworth is known for his incredible fashion sense. He always wears the most immaculate suits. His everyday suit, pictured below, is visually stunning and class as can be. His first trial suit is also widely regarded as one of the best suits worn by a man.
